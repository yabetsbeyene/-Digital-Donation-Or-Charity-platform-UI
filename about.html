About HelpEthiopia

Connecting hearts, changing lives, building communities

Our Story

Vision

We envision a Ethiopia where no one goes without basic necessities, where communities support each other, and where technology bridges the gap between those who can give and those who need help.

Mission

HelpEthiopia connects generous donors with verified recipients across Ethiopia through a transparent, efficient, and dignified platform that ensures every donation reaches those who need it most.

Our Values

Transparency

Every donation is tracked from posting to delivery, ensuring complete visibility in the giving process.

Dignity

We treat all users with respect, ensuring that receiving help is a dignified experience for everyone.

Community

We believe in the power of community support and local connections to create lasting change.

How We Help

For Donors

  • Easy item posting with photos and descriptions
  • Direct connection with verified recipients
  • Track your donation's impact
  • Safe and secure platform

For Recipients

  • Post specific needs with urgency levels
  • Connect with local donors
  • Receive help with dignity and respect
  • Access to verified NGO partnerships

Our Impact Areas

Education

School supplies, books, and educational materials for students across Ethiopia.

Healthcare

Medical supplies, first aid kits, and health-related items for clinics and families.

Food Security

Food packages, canned goods, and nutritional support for families in need.

Clothing

Warm clothing, shoes, and essential garments for children and adults.
