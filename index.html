Our Mission

Transparency, Efficiency, Trust

HelpEthiopia connects donors with people in need across Ethiopia. We make sure donated items reach the right people in a simple and clear way.

Our goal is to reduce waste, improve transparency, and support communities through direct and meaningful donations.

How It Works

Simple steps to smart giving

1. Post It

Donors quickly list their items (food, books, electronics) with photos and location details.

2. Pin the Need

Recipients/NGOs post requests, marking their location and urgency level.

3. Smart Match

The platform matches nearby donations to active requests based on need and location.

4. Connect

Users connect directly to arrange safe delivery of items to recipients.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does HelpEthiopia work?

HelpEthiopia connects donors with verified recipients. Simply post what you want to donate or request what you need, and our platform helps match nearby opportunities.

Is it free to use?

Yes! HelpEthiopia is completely free for both donors and recipients. Our mission is to make giving accessible to everyone.

How do I verify my organization?

NGOs and organizations can contact us directly to begin the verification process. We review documentation to ensure legitimacy.
